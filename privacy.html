Who We Are

MIKAYLA ("we", "us", "our") operates the website mikaylaco.com — an AI-powered travel fashion intelligence platform providing city-specific outfit recommendations, trend sourcing, and shoppable outfit curation.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information about you when you use our website and services. By using MIKAYLA, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Questions? Contact us at: hello@mikaylaco.com

What We Collect

Information you give us

  • Email address — when you join our waitlist or request early access
  • Photos you upload — when you use the AI Outfit Scanner. These are processed in real time and are not stored on our servers beyond your scan session
  • City and budget preferences — selected during your session to personalise trend results

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, time spent, referring URLs
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • IP address — used for approximate geolocation (country/region level) and security
  • Cookies and similar technologies — see the Cookies section below

What we do not collect

  • Payment information — all purchases happen on third-party retailer websites
  • Account credentials or passwords — we do not require account creation
  • Precise GPS location data

How We Use It

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver city-specific trend recommendations and outfit suggestions
  • Process your AI outfit scan and return trend scores and shoppable alternatives
  • Send early access and product update emails (waitlist only — unsubscribe anytime)
  • Improve our AI models and recommendation quality
  • Analyse usage patterns to improve the platform
  • Detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or security incidents
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for targeted advertising.

Pinterest Integration

MIKAYLA uses the Pinterest API to display fashion inspiration imagery. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest, Inc.

We use Pinterest API v5 to search for and display publicly available Pins related to fashion, travel, and style. When displaying Pinterest content:

  • All Pins link back to their original source on Pinterest, as required by Pinterest's developer guidelines
  • We do not store Pinterest images on our servers — images are served from Pinterest's CDN
  • We do not modify, filter, or alter Pinterest content
  • We do not use Pinterest data to build advertising profiles
  • We do not collect Pinterest user data or require you to log into Pinterest
  • We do not create new content from Pins for redistribution

Our use of the Pinterest API is governed by the Pinterest Developer Terms of Service and Pinterest Developer Guidelines.

For information on how Pinterest handles data: Pinterest Privacy Policy →

AI Features

Outfit Scanner

When you use our optional AI Outfit Scanner and upload a photo, that photo is:

  • Sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis only
  • Used solely to identify clothing items and generate trend scores
  • Not stored on our servers after your session ends
  • Not used to train AI models without separate explicit consent
  • Not shared with any third party other than Anthropic for processing

Using the scanner is entirely optional. All trend feeds, outfit building, and shopping features work without uploading any photo.

Trend Intelligence

Our AI trend engine uses Claude to generate city-specific fashion analysis. This does not involve any personal data — it uses general fashion knowledge and public web sources.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies and browser storage:

Essential functionality

  • Session memory to retain your city selection and preferences within a browsing session
  • In-memory trend data caching for performance — this does not persist after you close your browser

Analytics

We do not currently use third-party analytics. If introduced in future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate notice and opt-out options.

Third-party cookies

Google Fonts and Pinterest's CDN may set cookies when their content is loaded. We do not control these — refer to their respective privacy policies above.

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some features.

Data Storage & Security

  • Waitlist emails are stored in Supabase with access controls and encryption at rest
  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Uploaded photos are processed in memory and not written to persistent storage
  • Waitlist emails are retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Cached trend data (non-personal) is automatically purged after 24 hours

No internet transmission is 100% secure. While we take reasonable precautions, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

All users

  • Unsubscribe — use the unsubscribe link in any email, or email us directly
  • Deletion — request removal of your email address at hello@mikaylaco.com

EU / UK users (GDPR)

  • Right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection
  • Our legal basis for processing your email is consent, withdrawable at any time
  • Our legal basis for usage data is legitimate interests in operating our service

California users (CCPA)

  • Right to know what we collect and how it is used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of sale of personal information — we do not sell personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

To exercise any right, email hello@mikaylaco.com. We respond within 30 days.

Children

MIKAYLA is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. In accordance with Pinterest's developer guidelines, our platform is not intended for use by children under 13.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact hello@mikaylaco.com and we will delete it promptly.
