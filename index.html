Our Story

Every City Has a
Dress Code.

MIKAYLA is a travel fashion intelligence platform built on one belief: where you're going should shape what you wear. Not generic packing lists — real, current, location-specific style guidance powered by AI.

We pull live data from fashion publications, social media trends, and local style culture to generate outfit recommendations that are actually relevant to the city you're visiting, right now.

Founded by a Canadian traveller who was tired of landing in a new city feeling underdressed — MIKAYLA is built for the fashion-forward traveller who wants to look like she belongs, wherever she lands.

"To make every traveller feel at home in the world's most stylish cities — one outfit at a time."
